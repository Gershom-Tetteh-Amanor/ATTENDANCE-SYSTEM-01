Your lecturer displays a QR code when they start a session. Point your phone camera at it to open the check-in page.

How it works

  • Lecturer starts a session and shows the QR code
  • Scan it — your phone opens the check-in page
  • Register with your fingerprint/face (first time)
  • Verify with fingerprint/face for each check-in
  • Your check-in appears on the lecturer's screen instantly
