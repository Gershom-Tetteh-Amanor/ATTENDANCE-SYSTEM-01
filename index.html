How to Check In

  • Point your phone camera at the QR code displayed by your lecturer
  • You will be taken to the check-in page automatically
  • Use your fingerprint or face recognition to verify (not your PIN)
  • Your attendance will be recorded instantly
⚠️ Important
You must be physically present in the classroom and use your fingerprint/face to verify.
